# Product Vision: TalentFlow – AI Talent Pipeline for SMBs

## 1. Vision Statement
For **HR managers in SMBs** who **struggle with talent acquisition**, **TalentFlow** is an **AI sourcing platform** that **predicts fit from resumes**. Unlike **LinkedIn**, our product **uses proprietary ML on internal data for 2x faster hires**.

## 2. Core Pillars
- **Pillar 1: Prediction** – Fit scoring (Emotional: uncertainty → clarity).
- **Pillar 2: Automation** – Auto-outreach (Emotional: tedium → efficiency).
- **Pillar 3: Insights** – Retention forecasts.

## 3. Moat & Differentiation
- **Type:** Switching Costs.
- **Description:** Deep CRM integrations. Beachhead: Tech SMBs in EU.
- **Defensibility Score:** 7/10 (Regulatory risks).

## 4. Success Metrics
- **North Star:** Time-to-hire < 30 days (Lenny benchmark for HR tools).
- **KPIs (SMART):**
    - Conversion rate: > 25% (Relevant to churn, time-bound Q4 2026).
- **Counter-Metric:** False positives < 10%.
